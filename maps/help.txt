Here are the available commands in the game, check before starting~
You can press Enter to commit the command.

MOV
This will make the bot move forword.
TL
This will make the bot turn left.
TR
This will make the bot turn right.
JMP
Make the bot jump to the next cell in the map.
LIT
Lit the light up at the current position of the bot.

For more details, see README.md in the root folder.
